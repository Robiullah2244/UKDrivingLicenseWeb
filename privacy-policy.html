Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Jan 14, 2026

Our driving license learning app is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines the information we collect and how it may be used.

Information Collection and Use

Our app offers users the option to log in using their Google account, or Apple ID. This login functionality is provided to:

  • Track and sync study progress across sessions or devices

The information collected through these login methods (such as authentication tokens) is used solely for these purposes and is not shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses third-party services to serve advertisements and collect usage analytics. These services may collect and process data such as:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., IDFA/GAID)
  • App usage data (e.g., session durations, screen views)
  • User interactions with ads

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob – for displaying ads and handling consent under the EU User Consent Policy

Consent and Data Transparency

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), the UK, and Switzerland, we use Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) SDK to request your consent for storing and accessing information on your device (IAB TCF Purpose 1).

If you give consent, it also applies to analytics purposes. This allows Firebase Analytics to track user behavior, such as screen views and session activity, to help us improve the app. Consent signals are automatically shared with Google services under Google’s Consent Mode.

You may change or withdraw your consent at any time through the app settings.

Premium (Monthly) — Subscription Terms

Premium (Monthly) is an auto-renewable subscription.

  • Payment will be charged to your Apple ID at confirmation of purchase.
  • The subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • Charges may occur up to 24 hours before the renewal date.
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription in iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
  • Refunds are handled by Apple and are subject to Apple's eligibility decisions.
